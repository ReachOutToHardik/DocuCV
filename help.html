Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a resume?

Click "Get Started" or "Build Resume" to access the resume builder. Enter your information, choose a template, and let our AI enhance your content. Download your PDF when ready.

Is my resume ATS-friendly?

Yes! All our templates are designed to pass Applicant Tracking Systems. We use LaTeX for perfect formatting that ATS systems can easily parse.

Can I edit my resume after downloading?

Yes, your resume is saved to your account. You can edit and download it as many times as you need with a Pro subscription.

What's included in the free plan?

The free plan includes 1 resume, basic templates, and PDF download with a watermark. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited resumes, all templates, AI writing, and no watermarks.

How does the AI writer work?

Our AI analyzes your input and transforms it into professional, achievement-focused bullet points using industry best practices and the STAR method.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods through our secure payment processor.
