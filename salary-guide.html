Salary Negotiation Tips

  • Research market rates for your role and location using sites like Glassdoor and Levels.fyi
  • Consider the total compensation package, not just base salary
  • Wait for the employer to make the first offer when possible
  • Always negotiate - most employers expect it
  • Be prepared to justify your ask with specific achievements and market data
  • Consider non-salary benefits like remote work, equity, and professional development
  • Get everything in writing before accepting

Factors Affecting Salary

Company Size

Large tech companies often pay more but startups offer more equity
