Master your next interview with confidence

Common Interview Questions

Tell me about yourself

Craft a compelling 2-minute pitch that highlights your background, key achievements, and why you're interested in this role.

What are your strengths and weaknesses?

Choose strengths relevant to the job and frame weaknesses as areas you're actively improving.

Why do you want to work here?

Research the company thoroughly and connect their mission and values to your career goals.

Describe a challenging situation

Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to structure your response with specific examples.

Interview Tips

  • Research the company and role thoroughly before the interview
  • Prepare specific examples of your achievements using the STAR method
  • Practice your answers out loud, but don't memorize them
  • Prepare thoughtful questions to ask the interviewer
  • Dress appropriately for the company culture
  • Arrive 10-15 minutes early (or log in early for virtual interviews)
  • Send a thank-you email within 24 hours
